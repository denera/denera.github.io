About Me

Passionate computational scientist specializing on large‐scale gradient‐based optimization algorithms and their applications in scientific discovery, engineering design, artificial intelligence and machine learning problems. Extensive experience developing high‐quality scientific software on heterogeneous high‐performance computing systems, with significant contributions to large open source projects. Currently working at GraphCore as an AI Applications Specialist, helping customers solve challenging AI/ML problems using GraphCore IPUs and optimizing the poplar SDK to improve GraphCore IPU performance on MLPerf benchmark problems.
